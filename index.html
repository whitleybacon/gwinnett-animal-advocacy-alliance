We are their voice.

We are a coalition of dedicated and concerned citizens committed to ensuring the highest standards of animal welfare at our local shelter. Recognizing the critical role that shelters play in the lives of vulnerable animals, we have come together to address issues related to their management and operations at Gwinnett County Animal Shelter. Our mission is to find mutually beneficial ways for the shelter management team and the community members to come together and work on the crisis as a team.

Through community engagement, open dialogue, and informed advocacy, we strive to bring about positive change and ensure that the shelter upholds its responsibility to provide compassionate and effective care. We believe every animal deserves a safe and supportive environment, and we are united in our efforts to make this a reality.
