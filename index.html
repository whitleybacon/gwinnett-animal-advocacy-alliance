Who We Are

Community advocates working for better animal welfare outcomes.

Gwinnett Animal Advocacy Alliance is a community-driven advocacy organization focused on transparency, accountability, public engagement, and policies that improve animal welfare throughout Gwinnett County.

We believe informed residents and constructive advocacy can help create better outcomes for animals, shelter staff, volunteers, rescues, and the community as a whole.

Learn More About GAAA

Our Focus

  • Transparency in animal welfare decisions
  • Accountability from county leadership
  • Public education and community engagement
  • Practical policy solutions for lasting change

Every animal deserves a voice.

Together we can create a more transparent, accountable, and compassionate future for animals in Gwinnett County.
