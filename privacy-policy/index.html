Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Gwinnett Animal Advocacy Alliance ("GAAA," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you provide through this website.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City and state
  • Information submitted through contact forms, volunteer applications, surveys, petitions, or advocacy campaigns

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process volunteer applications
  • Send updates about advocacy efforts, events, and campaigns
  • Coordinate community initiatives
  • Improve our website and services
  • Track participation in advocacy actions

Email Communications

If you choose to provide your email address, we may contact you regarding:

  • Animal welfare advocacy efforts
  • Legislative action alerts
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Organizational updates

You may unsubscribe from email communications at any time.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

We may share information:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers that help operate our website or communications
  • When you voluntarily participate in campaigns that direct messages to government officials or public agencies

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site and improve user experience.

You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies.

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through our website. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
