Volunteers play a critical role in improving the lives of shelter animals. From walking dogs and socializing cats to helping with events and adoptions, volunteers provide enrichment, exercise, and human connection that animals desperately need.

Help Shelter Animals

Volunteers provide exercise, enrichment, and socialization that help reduce stress and improve quality of life for animals awaiting adoption or rescue.

Make a Difference

Every walk, photo, social media post, and interaction can help an animal find a home or attract rescue support.

Support Your Community

Strong volunteer programs benefit animals, shelter staff, adopters, and the entire community.

Interested in Volunteering?

Gwinnett County Animal Shelter manages its own volunteer program, including applications, orientations, training requirements, and volunteer assignments.

If you are interested in becoming a shelter volunteer, we encourage you to visit the official shelter volunteer page and begin the application process.
