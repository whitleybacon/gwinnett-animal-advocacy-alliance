Volunteer at the Shelter

Volunteers provide enrichment, exercise, socialization, and lifesaving support for shelter animals.

Help Shelter Animals

Interested in Volunteering?

Gwinnett County Animal Shelter manages its own volunteer program, including applications, orientations, training requirements, and volunteer assignments.

If you are interested in becoming a shelter volunteer, we encourage you to visit the official shelter volunteer page and begin the application process.
