How You Can Help

Your involvement ensures that the animals of Gwinnett County receive the advocacy and care they deserve.

Shelter Status Monitoring

We are currently tracking animals at the Gwinnett County Animal Shelter to ensure transparency. Specifically, we monitor animals in status categories such as "Vet Care/Post Op" and "Awaiting Spay/Neuter."

Our goal is to follow these animals through the system and pull public records to ensure proper care and outcomes.

Advocacy & Outreach

Help us attend local council meetings and speak up for animal welfare legislation. We provide the data; you provide the voice.

Volunteer & Foster

Directly support local rescues by offering your time or opening your home to a pet in transition.
