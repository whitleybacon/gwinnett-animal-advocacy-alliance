Why Public Participation Matters

Animal Advisory Council meetings are one of the few public forums where animal welfare issues are discussed in an official county setting.

Community participation helps ensure that concerns affecting animals, residents, rescues, volunteers, fosters, adopters, and taxpayers remain visible.

Meaningful change rarely happens because one person speaks once. It happens when residents stay informed, engaged, and persistent over time.
