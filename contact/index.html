Contact GAAA

Have a question, concern, or information related to animal welfare in Gwinnett County? Use the form below to contact Gwinnett Animal Advocacy Alliance.

Please note: GAAA is not a shelter, rescue organization, foster network, or animal control agency. We are unable to take in animals, rehome pets, pick up strays, provide emergency animal services, or respond to urgent shelter placement requests.

If your message is related to advocacy, public records, county policy, community concerns, or ways to get involved, we would be glad to hear from you.
