Issues & Advocacy

We advocate for practical, humane solutions that improve outcomes for animals, support responsible pet ownership, and strengthen animal welfare throughout Gwinnett County.

Additional Areas We Care About

Animal welfare extends beyond shelter intake numbers. We support efforts that improve quality of life, increase lifesaving capacity, and create sustainable solutions.

Medical Care

Timely veterinary care and treatment can significantly improve outcomes for animals awaiting adoption, rescue, or reunification.

Shelter Enrichment

Mental stimulation, exercise, and positive human interaction help reduce stress and improve overall well-being.

Foster & Rescue Partnerships

Strong partnerships expand capacity, create additional placement options, and help save more lives.

Creating Change Takes Community Support
