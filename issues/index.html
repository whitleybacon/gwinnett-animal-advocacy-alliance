Issues & Advocacy

Gwinnett Animal Advocacy Alliance works to improve outcomes for animals through education, community engagement, policy advocacy, and practical solutions that address both immediate needs and long-term challenges.

Our Focus Areas

We advocate for humane, effective, and sustainable solutions that improve the lives of animals and strengthen our community.

Additional Areas of Concern

Animal welfare extends beyond shelter intake numbers. We believe every animal deserves proper care, enrichment, and opportunities for a positive outcome.

```

Medical Care

Access to timely veterinary treatment and appropriate medical support can significantly improve outcomes for shelter animals.

Shelter Enrichment

Mental stimulation, exercise, and positive human interaction help reduce stress and improve quality of life for animals in care.

Foster & Rescue Partnerships

Strong partnerships expand lifesaving capacity and create additional pathways for animals in need.

```
