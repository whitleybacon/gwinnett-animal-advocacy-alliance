Our Mission TEST

The Gwinnett Animal Advocacy Alliance is dedicated to improving the lives of local animals through community education, policy advocacy, and direct support. We believe every animal deserves a safe and loving environment.

What We Do

  • Promote local animal welfare legislation.
  • Support local shelters and rescue organizations.
  • Educate the public on responsible pet ownership.
  • Coordinate community foster programs.
